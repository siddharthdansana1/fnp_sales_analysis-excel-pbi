##  Project Overview
This project analyzes sales performance data of **Ferns & Petals (FNP)** ‚Äî one of India‚Äôs leading gifting and flower delivery brands.  
Using data from multiple sources, the goal was to understand **sales trends**, **top-performing products**, and **customer purchasing patterns** across different occasions and cities.

The analysis was done using **Microsoft Excel** for data cleaning and **Power BI** for creating an interactive dashboard.

---

##  Business Problem
The company needs to analyze its sales data to identify top-performing products, profitable regions, and customer purchasing patterns.  

This analysis helps FNP to:
- Track and improve sales across key occasions and cities.  
- Identify high-performing product categories.  
- Understand customer behavior and average spending trends.  
- Make data-driven marketing and product decisions.

---

## Dataset
Three datasets were used in this project:
1. **Orders.csv** ‚Äì Details of order ID, order date, city, amount, and product ID.  
2. **Products.csv** ‚Äì Product names, categories, and prices.  
3. **Customers.csv** ‚Äì Customer IDs, demographics, and spending details.

##  Data Cleaning & Preparation
Data cleaning was performed in **Excel** before importing to Power BI:
- Removed duplicate and blank rows  
- Handled missing values in key columns (city, price)  
- Standardized date formats for time-based analysis    
- Normalized inconsistent category names  
- Filtered invalid records (negative or zero amounts)

##  Research Questions & Key Findings
1. **Which occasions generate the most revenue?**  
   ‚Üí Anniversary, Raksha Bandhan, and Holi drive more than 50% of total revenue.  
2. **Which cities have the highest orders?**  
   ‚Üí Imphal and Dhanbad are top performers.  
3. **What categories perform best?**  
   ‚Üí Colors and Sweets dominate total sales.  
4. **When do customers spend the most?**  
   ‚Üí During festival months and midday hours.  
5. **Which products need improvement?**  
   ‚Üí Plants and Mugs have potential for targeted promotion.

##  Final Recommendations
- **Boost marketing** during underperforming occasions (Diwali, Valentine‚Äôs Day).  
- **Promote low-performing but high-margin products** like Plants and Mugs.  
- **Target campaigns** in high-order cities (Imphal, Dhanbad, Kavali).  
- **Use time-based discounts** during peak hours (10 AM‚Äì1 PM).  
- **Expand product range** in top-selling categories (Colors and Sweets).
